The given chart represents the number of users and means of usage of Facebook between 2012 and 2013 as well as different reasons for usage of both men and women. First of all, it is important to mention the shift from personal computers to mobile phones over the given period. In March 2012 around 150 million users accessed Facebook via desktop, while a year later that number decreased to less than 140 million. On the other hand, the number of mobile users rose significantly from around 60 million in 2012 to almost 100 million in 2013. According to the conveyed survey, most people use Facebook to share media content although women are by 15% more keen on than men. Second most important reason was receiving updates where 39% of both women and men considered it significant. The consumption of comedic content was by 4% more popular among women than updates, and by the same 4% less popular among men. Least important Facebook's aspect was found to be learning about ways to help others: only 35% of female and 25% male respondents answered this was their reason to attend the platform.
