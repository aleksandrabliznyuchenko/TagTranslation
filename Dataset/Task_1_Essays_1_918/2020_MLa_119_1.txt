The bar chart shows the number of Facebook users in march 2013 much lower than in 2012. And this is observes only in desktop users. At the same time, users of mobile version of the site increased dramatically. If we talk about the reason for using this site, then we can identifya strong gender difference. For example, men are less likely to use facebook for sharing photos and videos. There are only 39% of them. While more than half of women do it. But there is something that men and woman do in the same amount - this is receiving updates.
We can clearly conclude that users are choosing a more mobile version of the site. And women do it much more often than men.
