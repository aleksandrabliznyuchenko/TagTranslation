The first bar chart demonstrates the number of users of Facebook with the help of mobile and desktop in millions between 2012-2013. The second one illustrates the primary causes for using this social media by males and females.
It is clear that more often people used desktop for Facebook and the most popular reason for it was sharing photos.
In 2012 the difference between using desktop and mobile was about 90 millions, but in 2013 the distance between them was much lower. It is obvious that in both years the most often users of Facebook were women, whose percentage of taking up was by 10-15 percent higher than men's.
