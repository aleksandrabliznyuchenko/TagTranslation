The bar charts show information about Facebook usage during the 1 year period from 2012 to 2013 and the reasons why people use it.
Overall, what stands out from the graph is that the quantity of both desktop and mobile users was more than one hundred million. In general there were more than 120 millon of desktop users and more than 40 million mobile phones users.
Looking at the details, the main reason for using this social network is sharing photos and videos. The percentage is about 54 for women and 39 for men. Whereas the least popular reason is learning about ways to help other peple between 25 percent for women and 35 percent for men. Other two reasons have almost the same percentage about 40 for both men and women.
