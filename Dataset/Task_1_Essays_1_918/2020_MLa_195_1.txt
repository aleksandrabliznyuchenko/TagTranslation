This bar chart shows the information about using a Facebook in period of 2012-2013 among men and women. In march 2012 about 150 millions of people used desktop, while in 2013 this amount decrease to approximately 138 millions of people. At the same time the amount of mobile users is increased from 58 millions to 99 millions in 2013.
Than, we can compare most important reasons for using facebook among men and women. Firstly, women more often sharing their photos or videos in comparison with men - 54 percent and 39 percent respectively. The amount of receiving updates is absolutely equal. Seeing funny posts and learning about ways to help others are more remarkable reasons for women than for men.
To sum up, as we can see in this bar chart, the consumption of facebook gradually increase but it is changed the way of scrolling facebook account to mobile instead of desktop.
