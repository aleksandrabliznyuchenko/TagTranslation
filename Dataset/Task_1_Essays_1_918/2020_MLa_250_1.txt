The graphs deal with changes in use of Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and reasons for using this social network among men and women. According to the first chart there were about 205 millions of Facebook users in 2012 and about 230 millions of users in 2013. It is evident that the quantity of users rised during the year of 2012. It is interesting that the quantity of mobile users steadly increased in 2013 but the number of desktop users decreased. This fact means that mobile phones became more and more popular. The second graph is about reasons for usinhg Facebook among men and women. According to the graph women prefer to use Facebook for sharing photos or videos. Also women are likely to look for some funny posts in Facebook. Both sharing photos or videos and receiving updates are popular reasons for using Facebook among men. Finally there is a strong correlation between increasing a popularity of Facebook and a number of mobile users.
