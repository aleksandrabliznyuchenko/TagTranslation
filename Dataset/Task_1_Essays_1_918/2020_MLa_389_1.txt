The given bar charts compare the usage of Facebook by men and women in a one-year period from March 2012 to March 2013 and shows the main reasons for that.
Overall, it can be seen from the graphs that people were more likely to use their desktop for checking their social media accounts, rather than opening the apps from their mobile phones, which is relevant to the whole period of time. In terms of details, it is obvious, that women were more active than men in all aspects of using Facebook, except for recieving updates. For insatnce, 54 percent of the females were sharing photos and videos, in comparison to the 39 percent of the male users. Moreover, women kept the leading position for both: seeing funny posts and learnig about ways to help others with 43 and 35 percent, while the figures for men in those aspects were 35 percent and 25, respectively.
