The bar chart shows how using of Facebook was changed between 2012 and 2013 period by desktop and by mobile phone and some reasons for using it among men and women. As is seen in the bar chart, the proportion of using Facebook by phone in 2012 was less, than it was with computer. By 2013 this situation incredible changed. Using Facebook on mobile became more popular. Moreover, the most popular reason to use such social network was sharing photos among all people.
The second occasion to use Facebook was seeing funny posts among female (43%). while male equally prefer learning about ways to help others and seeing funny posts (43%). Receiving updates also has big part in people's life, it is 39% for women and men.
To sum up, the size and distribution of Facebook in people's life was significantly changed, became more and more popular over the period.
