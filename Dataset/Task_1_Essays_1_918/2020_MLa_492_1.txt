The first given bar chart shows the distribution of Facebook accounts by devices used compared 2012 and 2013, while the second chart specifies particular reasons of males and females for using Facebook.
Looking more closely at the first chart, it is noticeable that in both 2012 and 2013 people prefer to use Facebook via computers.
