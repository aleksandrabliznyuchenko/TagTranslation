It is universally accepted that some of the individuals believe that watching a particular sport in TV or at live sports events is just killing precious time. However, i completely disagree with the statement as in my opinion it is actually a usefil activity and has many benefits of its own.
On the one hand, by watching sports in TV or at sports events, we can learn several things such as cooperation and team spirit. Sports teams demonstrate a tremendous level of team spirit that we can apply in ours daily life. Moreover, while watching the game, we can gain immense knowledge about it. Furthermore, watching sports with our loved relatives is a great opportunity to spend some qualiti time with them. Better still, cheering together for our favorite team brings us closer. Hence, watching sports offers many benefits that are much needed in every sphere of life. On the other hand, people who are intersted in sport, they often have role models that fascinate them the most. It is important to notice that one tries to copy them and follow their secrets to stay fit and intimately gets benefited by adopting healthy lifestyles. Some of them get inspired and choose sports as their career. For example, the football player of French team Mbappe was inspired by Crisiano Ronaldo and dreamt of becoming like him. With substantial amount of dedication and hard work he acheived it. So, there are many such stories where a particular sport has inspired people.
In conclusion, watching sports is a good way of spending time and for some people this hobby may even turn into career. So, people should encourage their children to develop interest in sport by watching it and participating in it whenever possible.
