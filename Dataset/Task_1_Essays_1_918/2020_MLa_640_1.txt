The charts illustrate the comparison between male and female groups using Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and also provides reasons for it.
Overall, the use of Facebook has declined over the year among both groups, but the statistics among women show that the fall has been much more significant. However, men use Facebook more often than women in 2013 (respectively 100 mln and 60 mln).
Speaking about the reasons, women are more eager to use Facebook for sharing photos, seeing funny posts and learning about the ways to help others. Moreover, men tend to be less active on the platform. But both groups account to the same percentage when it comes to receiving updates (relatively 39% and 39%).
In conclusion, women are more involved in social media activities and they find more reasons for doing so. On the other hand, men are less likely to be concerned with that.
