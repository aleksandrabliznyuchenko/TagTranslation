The charts represent information about the usage of Facebook between 2012 and 2013 and why men and women use this social network.
Overall, it can be seen that the number of people using desktops to search the Facebook site is higher than the number of people using mobile phones. The most popular reason (for both genders) for using this platform is sharing photos or videos.
Looking at the details, the number of desktop users slightly decreased from around 150 millions of people to approximately 139 millions of people. While the number of mobile users showed an upward trend, growing from approximately 59 millions of people to 99 millions. The most popular reasons for using Facebook among women are sharing photos and videos (54%), seeing funny posts (43%) and receiving updates (39%). While among men the most popular categories are sharing photos and videos (39%), receiving updates (39%) and seeing funny posts (35%).
