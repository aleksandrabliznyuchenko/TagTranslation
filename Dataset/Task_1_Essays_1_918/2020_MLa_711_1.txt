The bar charts compare changes in ways and reasons of using facebook. Charts provide information about women and men over a year from march 2012 to march 2013.
Overall, the most significant trend is decrease the number os people, who use computers and increase mobile users. Moreover, women are more interested in humanitarian aspect, while men in technical updates.
Number of desktop users fell from 143 millions of users to 139 millions. Going to mobile users, the number increase from 59 to 99 millions respectively.
The most popular reasons for using facebook for women is sharing and seeing content in the Internet (about 54% and 43% respectively). Less popular one is learning about ways to help others (about 35%). Men are interested in receiving updates (about 39%).
