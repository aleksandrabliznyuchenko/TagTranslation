The chart gives information about popular devices for using Facebook in 2012 and 2013 and also reasons for it among men and women.
Overall, it can be seen that the popularity of this social network has fallen by 2013. However, mobile usage of Facebook began to prevail over the desktop.
Most of women (54%) prefer Facebook as a social network where they can share photos and videos, while the highest percent of men (39%) share both receiving updates and sharing function of this website. It is also 39% of women who claim that the main reason for using Facebook is receiving updated. The less popular function of this social network for both men and women is learning about ways to help others (25% for men and 35% for women). In the same time, 35% of men chose a function of seeing funny posts as their favourite. 43% of women agreed with them.
