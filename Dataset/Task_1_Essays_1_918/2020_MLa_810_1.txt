Why and how much people use Facebook.
A study conducted between March 2012 and March 2013 based on the difference in the use of Facebook between men and women, shows the two main activities carried out on Facebook by both genres can be divided into: Entertainment and Information. Study also shows that a change in the medium used to access Facebook has changed, in fact, during 2013 has been registered that a growing number of users (about 100 million) where using mobile phones instead of desktop, while in March 2012 users where still relying into desktop for their social network activities.
