The bar charges show us how the usage of Facebook were changing between 2012 and 2013 and the reasons for using it among male and female.
In March 2012 the desktop usage of Facebook outnumbered the desktop usage in 2013. The main reason for using Facebook is sharing photos/videos both by women and men. There is no gender gap in the receiving updates. Female users made up a particularly large proportion in sharing photos and videos.
There had been a notable increase in usind mobile phones for using Facebook between 2012 and 2013. In March 2012 around 55 millions of users compare to almost 100 millions in March 2013. There is also notable gap between men and women in learning about ways to help others. 35 per cent of women compare to only 25 per cent of men.
