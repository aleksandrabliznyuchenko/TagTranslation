The charts demonstrate transformations in the use of Facebook. The first bar chart is devoted to devices used to communicate via Facebook, while the second one hilights the reasons of using the above social network.
In the first bar chart two obvious tendencies can be observed: the amount of peope who use desktops decreased in 2013, while the amount of mobile phone users increased significantly.
When it comes to the second bar chart, sharing photos and videos stands at 54% for women, which is approximately 15% higher than the percentage of men the same category. Equal percentage of men and women uses Facebook to receive updates. Facebook is mostly used by women to watch funny videos and learn about ways to help others.
In general, the main purpose of using Facebook is sharing photos, men and women share their interest in receiving updates. Moreover, Facebook is often used to enjoy amusing content and to help others.
