The charts show the amount of users on Facebook between 2012 and 2013 year of both gender. Overall, comparison between two years demonstrates that more people used mobile phone for posting photos and charity.
Users of Facebook by desktop were slightly decreased from 145 million to 138 million. However, the amount of mobile users were grown approximately two-third. Reasons for opening Facebook were the same for male and female. Women usually open this website to show their life by photo and videos in 54%, thus it was interesting for them to saw funny photos in 43%. Furthermore, men did the same and use Facebook one-third to posted cards and saw funny posts. The same was only one position for receiving updates and 39% of audience answered like this.
Both charts illustrate that mobile phone is more comfortable for users of Facebook and they prefer to share with followers their life and get some happiness by the platform.
