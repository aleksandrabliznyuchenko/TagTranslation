The first vertical bar chart illustrate changes in using a social network, like Facebook, from 2012 to 2013. The second horizontal bar chart demonstrate the reasons why woman or man using Facebook. It measured in percentage.
At the beginning, it can be see that mobile phones were not very popular if we start to compare with desktop. However, the popularity of mobile has dramatically age in march 2013.
For the second chart, it is immediately evident that the most obvious reasons for ising facebook is to sharing photos and videos (54% for male and 39% for female). The second most important for woman is seeing funny posts while for men is receiving updates. The latter reason (Learning about ways to help others) is equally low for both sexes (35% and 25%).
Overall, we can see a clear trend that using facebook is polarizing for all genders for different reasons and is growing every year.
