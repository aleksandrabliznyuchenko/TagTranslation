The graph illustrates the differences between using social network which is called Facebook during 1 year and also analyzes why people use it.
Overall, it can be seen that the figure for Facebook is the highest in 2012 years. Also it is clear that women were more active users on social network than men. However, in 2013 the situation changed: about 100 million women versus 60 million men.
Analyzing the reasons for using the social network, it becomes obvious that the platform is very relevant for women: they use it for sending photo or videos, receiving updates, seeing funny posts and learning something new while men just need to exchange messages and get updates.
To conclude, the popularity of using Facebook has significantly decreased over the year, and this site is more suitable for meeting the needs of women.
