The first chart illustrates the differences between 2012-2013 years in using Facebook via desktops and mobiles, whereas the second one presents variable aims of having that social network.
It can be easily seen that most of users prefer logging into Facebook with the help of desktops in both years represented on the chart. Nevertheless, there is a significant rise in the amount of mobile users from 50 millions to almost 100 millions. However, desktops are still used much more frequent than other devices. The most popular reasons of using Facebook among women are sharing photos and seeng funny posts. On the other hand, men are interested in sharing content as well and receiving updates on the same level.
To sum it up, most of people use Facebook via desktops in both years while being excited to share some visual content and getting updates.
