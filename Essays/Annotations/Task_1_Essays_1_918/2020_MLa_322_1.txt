The charts illustrate tendencies in the usage of Facebook among both genders over the period of 1 year (from 2012 to 2013).
Overall, even though using of mobile dramatically became more popular, the number of desktop users just slightly decrease. Moreover, women turned out to be more active facebook users.
From the first chart we can clearly see that the number of desktop users fell from about 150 millions to almost 140. Growth of proportion of mobile users compound around 40 millions. In every reason from the second chart percentage of women was more than the percentage of men. For instance, the reason "sharing photos" has 54% of women and only 39% of men.
