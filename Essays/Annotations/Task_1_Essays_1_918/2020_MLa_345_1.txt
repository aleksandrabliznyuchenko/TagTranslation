The two bar charts illustrate how the usage of Facebook changed from March in 2012 to March in 2013 and the main causes to use this social network among men and women.
Generally speaking, usage of Facebook decreased and the main reason for using it between both genders is for sharing photos and videos.
For example, in March 2012 about 150 million people used Facebook on their desktops. By March 2013 this number fall and became approximately 135 millions of users. In contrast, use of Facebook on mobile phones rose up from 55 million users in 2012 to 95 millions of people in 2013. As for main reasons for using it, the least popular for men and women is learning about ways to help others (25% men, 35% women). 54 per cent of women and 39 per cent of men suggest that the main cause for them is sharing photos and videos. The reason about receiving updates has the same number of per cent for both genders (39%). As for seeing funny posts, 43 per cent of women and 35 per cent of men chose this option.
