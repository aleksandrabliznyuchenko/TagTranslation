The charts represent usage of Facebook in 2012 and 2013 in comparison and gender difference of why it is used. It is evident from the chart that using Facebook on mobile devices became more popular.
The figures show that use of Facebook on desktops declined by approximately 25 millions of users over the period from 2012 to 2013. In comparison figures for use of Facebook on mobile phones increased roughly by 2 times from 50 millions in 2012 to 100 millions in 2013.
Second chart shows reasons for using Facebook including sharing photos or videos, receiving updates, seeing funny posts and learning about ways to help others. The most popular reason for both men and women is sharing photos and videos. Figures show that 54% of men and 39% of women use Facebook for this reason. Receiving updates is the second popular reason and figures show that its is used by same percent of men and women. The least popular reason is learning about ways to help others and figures for it show that 35% of men and 25% of women use Facebook for this reason which is the least percentage of all reasons.
