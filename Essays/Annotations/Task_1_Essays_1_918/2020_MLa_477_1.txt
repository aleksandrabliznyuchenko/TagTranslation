The bar plot compares the numbers of desktop and mobile users of Facebook between the March of 2012 and the March of 2013. The second figure demonstrates the main purposes of having a Facebook account.
The number of desktop users has fallen, while there is a rise in the number of mobile users. Concerning the reasons to use, female users mainly use the social networking site (SNN) to share content, while males enjoy both sharing feed updates The number of desktop users has dropped to nearly 130 millions from 150 millions of users. The number of mobile users has experienced a two fold growth: up to almost 100 millions from more than 50 millions.
54% of women use Facebook to share videos and/or photos. In contrast, men use Facebook for two purposes: content sharing and feed updates. Both reasons are accounted for by 39% by male users.
