The given chart displays changes in the use of Facebook through the one year and the major reasons for using it.
It is clear, that with time passing by people tend to use mobile over a desktop to access Facebook. The main reason for women to use Facebook was to share photos or videos, when for men the most valuable activity to visit the site was seeing funny photos.
In fact, mobile phone usage increased almost twice since the March 2012 - it drastically grew from nearly 60 millions of users to the 95 millions of users, while the desktop usage was slightly decreasing and hit 135 millions users. For the majority of menreceiving updates and sharing photos had even top value as the main reason to visit Facebook - 39 percent. On the other hand, women found sharing photos more attractive than seeing funny posts - 54 percent for the fist statement and only 43 percent for the second.
