Nowadays there is a widely shared opinion, that watching sport on TV, (including live sport events), is a significant waste of time. However, there are others, who strongly disagree.
In my opinion, watching different sport programmes or live events, related to sport, on TV is not really a procrastinating way of spending time. Firstly, watching people, performing sport activities on a regular basis can increase the willing of a person to start exercising themselves. For example, seeing somebody's team winning and then celebrate it with teammates may encourage a certain boy or a girl to try to gain such a success and a level of satisfaction with his or her achievements in life.
