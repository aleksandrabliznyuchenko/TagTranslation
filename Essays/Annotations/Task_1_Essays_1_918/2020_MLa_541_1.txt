The bar charts illustrate the use of Facebook and the main reasons for using social network Facebook over a two year period (2012-2013) Overall, it is clear that the percentage of people using Facebook on desktop is dramatically higher than ones using it on mobile. However, in 2013 an increase of people using mobile app can be traced.
Talking about the reasons for using Facebook women are more bias to share photos and videos than men. (54% of women). Both men and women show little interest in receiving updates (39% as shown on the chart).
In contrast women are more likely to surf Facebook for funny posts which compiles to 43%. Likewise men are 10% less likely to go to Facebook to learn about ways to help others than women.
