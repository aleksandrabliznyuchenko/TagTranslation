The first graph shows some changes in the use of Facebook (social network) during the 2012-2013. The second chart below presents some reasons for using Facebook by men and women.
The first graph demonstrates the decreasing of the number of people who use the Facebook by desktop from 150 to 130 millions.
What about the mobile phones, the situation is different. The amount of people who surfing this social network increased significantly from 45 to 100 millions.
The most popular reason to use the Facebook for men and women is sharing photos or videos and the percentage is 39% and 54% respectively.
Other reasons are practically on the same level for both sexes for example receiving updates (about 39%). But it will necessary ro notice that women prefer see funny posts more than men.
