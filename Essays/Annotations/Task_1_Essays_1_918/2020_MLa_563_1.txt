The charts provide information about changes in usage of Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and the difference between reasons for using among women and men.
Overall, there was an increasing trend in mobile usage of Facebook from 2012 to 2013. For women and men the main reason of using Facebook was sharing photos or/and videos.
It is noticeable from the first chart, while desktop usage of Facebook fell down from almost 150 millions of users to 139 millions approximately, the mobile usage increased in one year from 60 millions to 100 in the one-year-period. Sharing photos or/and videos was the most popular reason for using Facebook for both genders. Second general reason was receiving updates, which had the same portion of percentage among men and women. The less popular reason was learning about ways to help others, it was only 35% among women and 25% among men.
