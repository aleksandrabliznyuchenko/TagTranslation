The bar charts represents the differences in the use of the Facebook between 2012 and 2013. Also, it gives information about the main reasons for using this social network.
Overall, use the Facebook from Desktop experienced a downward trend, while mobile showed an upward trend throughout the period.
To start with, approximately 150 millions of people used desktop at 2012 and about 55 millions used mobile respectively. Then, mobile use experienced a significant rise and grew to nearly 100 millions, while desktop had a downward trend and was about 140 millions at the end of the period.
