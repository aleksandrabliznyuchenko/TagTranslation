The given line graph shows why people use Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and how it changed.
Overall, the situation was unstable throughout the period.
In March 2012 about 150 millions of people used Facebook on desktop, while on mobile only 50 millions. In March 2013 about 135 millions of users used desktop, while only 90 millions of users used mobile. The main reasons for using Facebook among women were sharing photos. videos and seeing funny posts 54% and 43% respectively. In comparison the main reasons among men were sharing photos. videos and receiving updates 39% and 39% respectively. as a result women use Facebook for Sharing photos and videos, while men for receiving updates and also for sharing photos. videos
