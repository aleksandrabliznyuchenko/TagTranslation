The first bar graph below presents some data on Facebook usage among people worldwide between 2012 and 2013. As is obvious, the mobile phone use has significantly increased over the period - almost 20 million new accounts, while desktop website got even less attention in a year.
Although over half of the Facebook users considered photo and video sharing as the main reason of social networking, the proportion in this category has decreased from 54 percent in 2012 to 39 persent in 2013, which may be a consequence of increased app functional.
In contrast, the numbers who reported receiving new updates as the prior reason has not changed during a year - only 39 percent of users.
