The represented graph gives the information about amount of people using Facebook since 2012 till 2013 and causes of using among women and men.
Generally, according to the graph it is evident that the users base shorted, however the popularity of mobile app rosed and the main reason for using Facebook is sharing photos or videos.
In detail, the amount of people using a desktop on March 2012 was 150 million of people, but in 2016 it reduced to just almost 60. If we talk about mobile app it's popularity shorted from 130 to 90. The main reason of using this social network and the popularity of mobile app is ability of sharing photos and videos (54% among women and 39% among men). Second one is receiving updated (equal for women and men - 39%), and the next place takes seeing funny post (43% for women and 35% for men).
