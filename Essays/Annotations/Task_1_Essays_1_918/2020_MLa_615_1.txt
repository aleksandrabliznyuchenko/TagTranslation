The bar chart shows the number of users of Facebook and what kind of tool did they use for this, over the period 2012 to 2013.
We can see that in March 2012 people used computers more then mobile phones but after a year the indicators change significantly and the phone comes out ahead. This is due to the development of smartphones in those years. Just in 2013 phones began to resemble portable computers.
We can see that women are more likely than men to visit Facebook to Sharing photos, seeing funny posts, learning about ways to help others. It can be concludet that women were more likely to use Facebook.
