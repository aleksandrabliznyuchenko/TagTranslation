The given bar charts depicts information on the main purposes, pursued by Facebook users, and technical means, used in order to get an access throughout the period between 2012 and 2013.
Overall, the number of users visiting Facebook with the help of their mobile phones has sufficiently increased, whereas the quantity of desktop-users has dramatically dropped.
It should be said, that the differences in main purposes for visiting the website, considered as main by men and women, are not as sufficient, as it could be expected in this period of time. Sharing photos or videos is treated as the main reason for using this social media primarily by women: log into their Fasebook accounts for this purpose.
