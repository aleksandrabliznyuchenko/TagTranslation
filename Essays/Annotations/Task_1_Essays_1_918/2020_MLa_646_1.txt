This charts dive the information about change in usage of Facebook social net between 2012 and 2013 and the main ways why people follow it.
In general, it can be seen the trend for the desk top was downward while there was an upward trend in the mobile phone usage. Talking about reasons, it can be noticed that women prefer use this social net for sharing photos and videos and seeing funny posts. Men come to Facebook mostly for sharing photos and videos like women, but they also use it for receiving updates.
To begin with, both men and women use Facebook to receiving updates with the same propotion 39%. The similar persentage can be noticed in men reason to use Facebook as sharing photos and videos. Moreover, 35% of male respondents prefer use Facebook for seeing funny posts while the same part of female respondents use it for learning about ways to help others.
