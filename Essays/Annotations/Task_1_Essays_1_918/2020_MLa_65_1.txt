The given charts demonstrate the difference between the usage of Facebook in the March 2012 and March 2013 and answer the question why do people of different genders use it.
It is clearly noticable that the amount of desktop users is much bigger than mobile users, but through the years the mobile usage has nearly doubled from 58 millions of users to 99 millions. On the contrary, the amount od the desktop users is decreased from 153 millions to 138 millions of users. Also, its hard not to mention that more than a half of a women use Facebook to share their photos or videos.
There is the same amount of female and male users open Facebook to receive their app updates. The least popular reason to use Facebook among all genders is to learn the ways to help other people and animals. It is only 35 percent of women and much less (25%) of men.
