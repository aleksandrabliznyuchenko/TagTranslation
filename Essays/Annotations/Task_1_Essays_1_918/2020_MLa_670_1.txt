The bar charts illustrate the information about using Facebook among people of different gender and the most popular reasons for doing it.
The first bar chart shows that the amount of Facebook users with a Desktop has not significantly changed. In contrast, the popularity of mobile version of Facebook has increased in March 2013 and was almost about 100 millions of users. While in March 2012 the most popular way of using was a desktop, in March 2013 the majority of people prefer using mobile phones.
The second bar provides information about the reasons for using this social media for men and women. The most popular reason among women is a need for sharing photos and videos and this percentage is about 54. It is clear that the equal importance for both men and women for using Facebook is receiving news and updates and was 39$. It is noticeable that the rarest reason for all people is learning about to help the society.
