The graph illustrates change of users' device preferences for facebook using. People were more likely to use a desktop for facebook in march 2012. In comparison, in march 2013 they were interested in using a mobile phone for this social media service. Using facebook in the desktop has no saturated changes in amount of users between this two years. But the amount of mobile users highly increased since 2012 till 2013. The second chart is about reasons why both men and women use facebook. Women like using facebook for sharing photos and videos, seeing funny posts and learning about ways to help others more than men like it. But both men and women have similar interest to receiving updates throw facebook. The smallest amount of men are interested in learning about ways to help others.
