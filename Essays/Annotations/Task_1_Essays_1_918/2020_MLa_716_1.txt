The presented charts explain the differences in Facebook usage from 2012 to 2013. In addition to that, pictures show the main exploitation reasons among males and females.
According to the Use of Facebook chart, overall the amount of all users decreased dramatically comparing both years. However, while the desktop usage shrank approximately by 20 million users, the quantity of mobile Facebook customers increased by 40 million.
Moving to the next chart, there is a significance to note that women mostly share photos or videos, while men tend to use the social media for seeing funny posts (54% and 43%, respectively). Both genders use Facebook for updates receiving in similar amounts (39%), while learning about ways to help others is the least popular among males (25%) and females (35%). It is also noteworthy that men use Facebook for seeing funny posts in the same amount that women use it for learning about ways to help others (35%).
