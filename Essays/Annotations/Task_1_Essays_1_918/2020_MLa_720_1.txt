The first bar chart illustrates how the Facebook was used for the period of two years and some primary reasons of its usage among women and men. The two graphs presents two types of technologies and four various categories of reasons.
It is obvious, that desktop is the most popular way for this social network for both years. There was a slight degree at a nearly 15 million of users in the march 2013. To contrast, the mobile was less used in march 2012 and by the march 2013 its popularity has grown from 50 to aproximately 100 million of users.
It is clear, that sharing photos and videos is the main reason for using Facebook among women, it counts 54%, whereas the most popular activity for men is seeing funny posts. The same percentage for both categories has a feature of receiving updates. Learning about ways to help others is more exciting for women than for men.
