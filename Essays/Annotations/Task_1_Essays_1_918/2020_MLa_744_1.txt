The carts illustrate how the use of Facebook changed from 2012 to 2013 and what are the main reasons to use it among woman and man.
Overall, comparing with March 2012 the mobile Facebook use grew up. Woman have more reasons to use Facebook.
In a year the desktop Facebook use decreased on 20% from 150% to about 130%. The mobile use increase slowly but surely from about 60% to 80%.
The main reason for woman using Facebook is sharing photos or videos that takes more than the half - 54%. For men this is also one of the most important reasons. Other popular reason that takes equal % in both sex - receiving updates. This reason have 39%. The least important reason for men and women - "learning about ways to help others" - 35% for woman and 25% for man.
