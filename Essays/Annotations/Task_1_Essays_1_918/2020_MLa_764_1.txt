The bar charts bellow illustrate changes in the Facebook usage from March 2012 to March 2013 and there is also a chart that shows the main reasons in percentage of men and women Facebook usage.
The first chart shows the difference between March 2012 and March 2013 and which gadgets were used for Facebook surfing. It is clear that in March 2012 it was more common to use a desktop. The chart illustrates that more than 140 millions of users prefer desktop over the mobiles both in 2012 and 2013. If we look at the side with March 2013, we can see that mobiles are more popular in March 2013 than in 2012.
As for the main reasons, women usually shared photos and videos. That take 54% of answers, while men take only 39%.
To sum up, it is correct to say that women use Facebook more often than men.
