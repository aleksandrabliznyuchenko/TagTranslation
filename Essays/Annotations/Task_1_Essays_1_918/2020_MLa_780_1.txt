The bar charts demonstrate how using Facebook by male and female users changed between 2012 and 2013.
During one year, the features of using Facebook by desktop have not changed a lot. The amount of desktop users marginally plunged from 150 millions to 135 by the end of the year. Whereas the quantity of mobile users remarkably increase almost twice. It demonstrates growing popularity of using mobiles among social active people.
Speaking about the reasons for using Facebook, the chart shows the difference in preferences between genders. For example, females are definitely more interested in sharing videos and photos, seeing funny posts and learning different ways to help others than males. Perhaps, receiving updates equally reasonable for both males and females. This means that the most active part of social media users consists of females, while males are using Facebook mostly for formal goals.
