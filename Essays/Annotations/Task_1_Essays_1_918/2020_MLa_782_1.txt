These two charts below depict the changes of usage of social media Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and the most significant causes for using it between male and female genders.
To start with, it is obviously that in march of 2013 there are less Facebook's users on desktop than in march of 2012. While at the same period, there are more users on the mobile version of that social media. It is interesting that women prefer to share some photos or videos more than men, but the results are same about the reason: receiving updates. Obviously, women like to see funny posts and to learn about ways to help others more than men.
To sum up, female gender is more interested in facebook social media by many factors as like: sharing posts, seeing funny pictures and learning something new. So, facebook become more popular for mobile in march of 2013, and become less popular on desktop.
