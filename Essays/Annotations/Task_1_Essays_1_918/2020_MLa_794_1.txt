The graphs illustrate movement in the use of Social Network such as Facebook in period of 2012 to 2013 and main trends describing reasons to use it among men and women.
Significant increase in mobile phone usage can be seen in this period followed by slight decrease in desktop category. Women and men interests also vary from one gender to another. Women have tendency to use Facebook for entertainment purposes more than men.
The numbers of millions of mobile users rocketed from 55 to almost 100 in a span of one year. In the same time, desktop users decreased from 150 to 130. 54% of women prefer to use Facebook for sharing photos and videos when in the same time only 39% of men use the social network for this. Moreover, both gender like to use it for receiving updates on the same level whis is 39%.
