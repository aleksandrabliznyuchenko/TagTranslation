The charts demonstrate the dynamic of using Facebook during one year with desktop and mobile and also show the percentage of reasons to do it by women and men.
Overall, it can be seen that the highest peak belongs to desktop during the whole year but there is an increase in using mobile comparing with March 2012. Moreover, the most popular reason for both genders is sharing photos and videos.
In March 2012 the amount of desktop users was around 150 millions while mobile users were almost 60 millions. However, the trend was changing during the year and people who use Facebook with mobile deviced rose in 40 millions.
In terms of men, the rate of sharing photo and videos, receiving updates and seeing funny posts is quite similar and do not have a big difference. At the same time women prefer more sharing photos and videos, seeing funny posts and the least important reason is learning about ways to help others.
