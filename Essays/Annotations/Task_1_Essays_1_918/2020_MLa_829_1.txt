As we can see from these two graphs, in 2012, Facebook was used mostly by desktop, around 150 million of users. At the same time, by mobile Facebook was used by almost 60 million of users. In 2013, the situation has changed. Users started to use more mobiles than desktop (90 million of users and 130 million of users respectively). Concerning who was using Facebook and what were the causes, women were using Facebook more than men. All the reasons for using Facebook such as, Sharing photos/videos, Receiving updates, Seeing funny posts and Learning about ways to help others, are showing that women were using Facebook almost on 10% everywhere. Only in Receiving updates the percentage between men and women were stable - 39%. The biggest gap between men and women in using Facebook was in Receiving photos/videos cause. The gap was 15%, which is really big.
