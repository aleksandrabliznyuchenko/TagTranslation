The charts above present the differences in numbers of Facebook users between 2012 and 2013 and the variety of Facebook usage purposes which differ between genders.
So, the first chart shows how devices used for logging in to Facebook changed and this change is not crucial. Whilst the amount of people using Facebook by dekstop dropped a little-there was 20 millions less users in 2013 than in 2012, more mobile phones were used for Facebook in 2013.
At the same time second chart shows that the reasons for Facebook usage are different for sexes. While females use Facebook more for sharing something, seeing funny posts and learning about ways to help others, the same number of males as females use it to receive updates.
All in all, it is clear that while the devices used to log in to Facebook change, the reasons for using this social network are differnet for genders.
