Two diagrams demonstrate how people use Facebook in 2012 and 2013 and what the causes for using this social network among women and men.
At close look the bar chart illustrates that people prefer using desktops than mobile phones. However, the amount of users who like mobile phones for Facebook is increased in 2013, whereas the desire to use a desktop becomes lower than in 2013. Men and women use Facebook differently. It is clear that women share photos / videos and see funny posts more often than men. It is interesting to notice that women learn about ways to help others more than men. The similar percentages among women and men have only the segment which names receiving updates.
Overall, the tendency to use mobile phones for Facebook grew in 2013, although the popularity of desktops are still higher. Women are more active users of Facebook than men.
