The charts illustrate the amount of Facebook's users on different gadgets and also various reasons for using this network among people of two sexes in 2012 and 2013. Units are measured in millions of users and percent.
Overall, using a mobile phone to communicate through Facebook became significantly more popular but still not as popular as using the desktop version. The main reason for choosing this social network was sharing media files for both men and women.
The percentage of mobile users increased from nearly 50 millions to almost 100. That might be considered as a drastic development. Meanwhile desktop version lost about 12 millions but remained the preferred option.
The second bar chart provided rather interesting information about various intentions for using Facebook. It turned out that women are more interested in posting photos, humour and learning how to help rhose in need than men. On the other hand, both males and females showed the same interest in being updated about different events and issues.
