The charts demonstrate us how the use of Facebook has changed over the years and what reasons for using it do people name.
Generally speaking, women see more reasons to use Facebook than men, moreover people started to use mobile version of Facebook more often during the given period.
The number of Mobile users started at approximately 57 million users in 2012 and then increased rapidly to 98 millions users, while the number of people, using desktop, slightly fell from 150 millions to 135 millions.
The most popular reasons to use Facebook are sharing photo and videos and seeing funny posts, they make up 54% and 43% relatively. Receiving updates makes equally 39% with both women and men. The least favourite reason to use Facebook is learning about ways to help other, it is only accounted for 35% and 25% by women and men relatively.
