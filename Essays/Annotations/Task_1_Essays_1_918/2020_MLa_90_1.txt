The first bar chart illustrates the amount of people who use desktop and mobile version of the Facebook between 2012 and 2013, while the second one shows the percentage of using Facebook reasons for male and female users. Overall, the most popular way of using Facebook is desktop with the results of 150 millions of users in March 2012 and approximately 135 millions of users in March 2013 and it is clear from the second bar chart that 54% of women or 39% of men choose the main reason for using Facebook is sharing photos or videos.
In March 2013, mobile shows 90% of usage. By contrast, in March 2012 mobile illustrates drop for 55%.
Seeing funny posts is the reason for 35% of men to use Facebook, while 39% of women choose receiving updates for the main reason. 25% of men use Facebook for learning.
